How does the bundle discount actually work?

As you add products, the discount tier rises automatically: 2 items = 5% off, 3 = 8%, 4 = 12%, 5 = 15%, 6 = 17%, 7 = 18%, 8 = 20%. At 6 items the free WhatsApp consult with Dr. Ahmad unlocks. At 7, a free Hyaluronic mini ships with your order. At 8 (the complete regimen), you also get a printed 28-day protocol card signed by Dr. Ahmad. Nothing to enter — the cart applies it live.

Is the dermatologist real, and is he reachable?

Yes. Dr. Muhammad Tauqir Ahmad is GMC-registered (the UK General Medical Council) and practiced dermatology in London before consulting on Clarté MD's formulations. The free consultation is delivered over WhatsApp by his Lahore-based dermatology associates; complex cases are escalated directly to him. Response window is 2 working hours, Mon–Sat, 10am–7pm PKT.

What if my skin reacts to a product?

WhatsApp us a photo within 14 days of receipt. Our consult team will adjust the routine (often the trigger is too-fast retinol introduction, easy to fix). If you'd rather return, we accept opened products for full refund within 30 days — unlike Daraz, which excludes opened cosmetics from its 7-day return window. Shipping refunds are calculated on actual cost.

Cash on Delivery — does it really mean no card needed?

Yes. Pick COD at checkout and you'll pay the courier in cash when your box arrives. No card details collected, no online payment required. We confirm the order over WhatsApp before dispatch — so if you change your mind, just tell us. Lahore arrives next-day; rest of Pakistan 2–4 business days via TCS.

How do I know the products aren't fake?

Every bottle is batch-stamped with a manufacture date, expiry date, and a QR code that resolves to its production record on clartemd.com.pk. If you ever receive a unit that fails verification, we refund 2× the purchase price — no questions, no return-shipping required. This is a guarantee imported brands sold by Daraz resellers structurally cannot match.
