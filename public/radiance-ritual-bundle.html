09 — Questions

The things everyone asks.

If you don't see your question, the WhatsApp team replies in under 10 minutes during clinic hours.

Is this bundle right for my skin type?

The Radiance Ritual is designed for normal, combination, and oily Pakistani skin with pigmentation, sun damage, or uneven tone. If you have very dry, eczema-prone, or actively inflamed skin, book the free WhatsApp consultation first — we may recommend the Clarity Daily Bundle instead.

How long until I see results?

Most patients see a reduction in dullness and improved texture by day 14. Pigmentation and melasma improvement typically appears between days 28 and 56, with steady continued change through day 90. Sun protection is the variable — without consistent SPF, the routine cannot work.

Can I pay cash on delivery?

Yes — COD is available across Pakistan. We also accept JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and all major credit/debit cards. Pre-paid orders ship 24 hours faster.

What's your return policy?

14-day returns on unopened product. If the product is opened and you've had a reaction, send a photo to the WhatsApp team — we'll exchange or refund. Authenticated-batch guarantee: if your bottle doesn't verify, we refund 2× the price.

Are the ingredients safe during pregnancy?

The retinol-containing products are not recommended during pregnancy or while breastfeeding. The Vitamin CE, Cleanser, and SPF are all pregnancy-safe. Book the free consultation and we'll recommend a pregnancy-appropriate routine.

How fast is delivery?

Lahore, Faisalabad, Islamabad, and Karachi: 24–48 hours. Other cities: 3–4 working days. Free nationwide shipping on this bundle.

How do I know this isn't fake?

Every bottle carries a batch QR. Scan it after delivery and our server returns the live record — MFG date, expiry, lab-tested actives. If your code doesn't return a record, send us proof and we refund 2× the price. This is the guarantee imported brands sold on Daraz physically cannot give you.
