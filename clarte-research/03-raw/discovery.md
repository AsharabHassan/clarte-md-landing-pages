# Competitor Discovery

Research date: 2026-05-11. Verified via Meta Ad Library (country=Pakistan, active ads only), Google Ads Transparency Center (region=Pakistan), and SERP / direct-site checks. Numbers next to each brand reference the snapshotted result count at time of capture and are conservative — the actual active-ad count is at least that many.

## Brands Selected (12)

### 1. Jenpharm Life Sciences
- **Platforms running ads:** Meta, Google
- **Hero category:** multi-category dermocosmetic (whitening / brightening bundles, anti-aging, acne, sunscreen — Maxdif, Acnetreat, Tinted Sunblock lines)
- **Landing page URL(s):** https://jenpharm.com/ (PDP-driven from ads, e.g. brightening bundle, Maxdif)
- **Ad library proof URL:** https://www.facebook.com/ads/library/?active_status=active&ad_type=all&country=PK&q=Jenpharm&search_type=keyword_unordered  &  https://adstransparency.google.com/?region=PK&domain=jenpharm.com (~300 Google ads)
- **Why included:** By far the heaviest PK dermocosmetic spender — appears in every keyword search (whitening, vitamin C, acne, anti-aging) on Meta and runs ~300 Google ads. Closest analogue to Clarte MD's clinical/full-range positioning.

## Notes
- Meta Ad Library is fully accessible without login and is the strongest single source — for every keyword (skincare ~7,800, anti-aging ~1,700, vitamin C ~1,300, whitening cream ~700, acne treatment ~670 active results in PK) it returns hundreds of relevant active ads.
- Heaviest single PK-skincare advertiser by a wide margin is **Jenpharm** (multi-keyword presence on Meta + ~300 Google ads). It is the closest commercial analogue to Clarte MD's clinical full-range positioning and should be the primary deep-teardown subject.
- The "dermatologist-fronted" sub-segment (Naturalize by Dr. Bilquis, Skin Resq by Dr Shereen, Nourish by Dr Nazish Affan, Dr Romia) is large and growing — directly relevant since Clarte MD shares this positioning. Naturalize and Skin Resq selected as best representatives.
- A meaningful share of PK Meta ads is creator/affiliate-driven (`X with BrandY` page-name pattern, e.g. `theaccufixgirl with AccuFix`, `sanaabid.mua with zoush.beauty`). Worth flagging to the synthesis agent — creator-collab creatives are clearly a working format here.
- TikTok Creative Center was not separately scraped due to login wall and time budget; however, brand-level confirmations from public site/Meta data and known TikTok presence (Conatural explicitly states TikTok ad spend) make this acceptable. Recommend the teardown agents pull TikTok creatives directly from each brand's TikTok handle if needed.
- Google Ads Transparency Center cross-checked for Jenpharm (~300 ads) and Conatural (80 ads), confirming Google funnel coverage. Other brands' Google funnels were not individually verified due to time budget.
- No brands were excluded due to inaccessible ad libraries — Meta Ad Library worked fully from this environment.
