# Conatural — Teardown

## 1. Brand Snapshot
- **Positioning:** Premium Pakistani clean-beauty house anchored on the tagline "Rooted in Nature, Powered by Science & Made with Honesty" — natural/organic formulations with science framing, the most aspirational of the local set.
- **Hero category:** Multi-category (skincare + haircare + body + makeup + baby), but skincare serums and hair bundles dominate the home merchandising.
- **Price tier:** Mid-to-premium local. Sample PKR prices: Anti-Acne Skin Serum Rs. 1,724; White Mask Rs. 3,105; Silk Foundation Rs. 4,945. They sit clearly above AccuFix/Jenpharm and noticeably below CeraVe/COSRX.
- **Origin claim:** Pakistan-based brand (PKR pricing, .pk operation under conaturalintl.com). The site does NOT state founder, founding year, factory location, or GMP/ISO certification on the pages reachable via WebFetch — a notable gap given their "Powered by Science" promise.
- **Where sold:** Own DTC at conaturalintl.com. Multi-channel presence indicated by nav references to "Partner With Us" and Instagram/WhatsApp, but no Daraz/retail partners surfaced on the home or story pages.

## 4. Bundle Economics (CRITICAL)
- **Does this brand sell bundles?** Yes — 35 bundles in the "Skin & Hair Care Bundles" collection. Bundling is a core merchandising pillar.
- **Bundle names + prices (top 5 by relevance to Clarte's hero-bundle play):**
  1. Hair Growth Bundle — Rs. 7,601 (was Rs. 8,446) — Hair Repair Shampoo + Conditioner + Intense Growth Hair Oil
  2. Super Growth Bundle — Rs. 6,431 (was Rs. 7,146) — Hair Growth Oil + Shampoo + Hair Repair Conditioner
  3. Rosemary Hair Care Trio — Rs. 5,289 (was Rs. 5,877) — Shampoo + Conditioner + Rosemary Essential Oil
  4. Ultra Skin Brightening Bundle — Rs. 4,346 (was Rs. 4,829) — Brightening Serum + White Mask + Brightening Skin Serum
  5. Vitamin C Kit — Rs. 2,654 (was Rs. 2,949) — Vitamin C Face Wash + Vitamin C Serum
- **Component math:** Every bundle observed shows the SAME flat 10% off bundle list price vs. summed individual price. Example: Vitamin C Kit Rs. 2,654 vs. Rs. 2,949 list = 10.0% saving. Hair Growth Bundle Rs. 7,601 vs. Rs. 8,446 = 10.0%. Hajj & Umrah Kit Rs. 1,553 vs. Rs. 1,725 = 10.0%. This is a uniform mechanical discount, NOT a perceived "good-better-best" curated value ladder — every bundle reads as the same deal.
- **Free shipping threshold (PKR):** "Free Shipping on all orders above PKR 4,999/-" — note this is engineered above the average single-product price (Rs. 1,148–1,724), so most bundles in the Rs. 3,000–4,500 range fall JUST under it, nudging an add-on.
- **Free gifts / mini sizes / samples:** Not present on the page.
- **Hero offer pushed in ads:** Site hero CTA is generic "SHOP NOW"; the most ad-distinctive bundle on the catalog is the **Hajj & Umrah Kit (Rs. 1,553, 10% off)** — a culturally timed hero with strong organic search demand, plus the perennial Hair Growth Bundle.
- **Subscription / repeat-purchase:** Not present on the page (no autoship/subscribe-and-save observed).

## 11. Clarte MD Strategic Implications
- **What to steal (2–3):**
  1. **The "Rs. 4,999 free-shipping threshold ≈ bundle price" trick.** Conatural deliberately sets the shipping line just above their key bundle prices so customers add a Rs. 500–1,000 unit to unlock it. Clarte's Rs. 5,000 Radiance Ritual Bundle should be priced to land *at or just over* a free-shipping line so the bundle itself unlocks the perk — a clean reason-to-buy that doubles as AOV defense.
  2. **Bundle taxonomy as a category in the nav.** "Bundles" is a top-level menu item with 35 SKUs — it is treated as a category, not a promo. Clarte should mirror this with a "Rituals" or "Bundles" nav slot from day one.
  3. **Culturally-timed hero kit (Hajj & Umrah).** Conatural built a defensible, search-rich, gift-able SKU off a Pakistani calendar moment. Clarte can replicate with a Wedding/Mayoun/Eid Radiance Ritual seasonal hero.
- **What to avoid (1–2):**
  1. **Uniform 10% discount on every bundle.** It teaches the customer that no bundle is special and that 10% is the floor — they will wait for a sale to get more. Clarte's bundles should vary 8–12% with the *headline* hero offer at 12% and curated/premium kits at 8%, framed as "value added" (free mini, free consultation, free derma sheet) rather than flat % off.
  2. **No trust stack.** Conatural carries badges (Halal, Cruelty-Free) but ZERO dermatologist, PMDC, GMP, ISO, or manufacturing transparency on pages we reached. For a "Clinical" positioning, Clarte must lead with what Conatural doesn't have: named dermatologist, manufacturing facility named, clinical-grade copy.
- **Whitespace threat:** Conatural sits **above** Clarte MD's "Premium Local Clinical" target on price (top serums Rs. 1,724–3,105; bundles Rs. 4,346–7,601 are in Clarte's Rs. 5,000 zone) but **below** it on clinical authority — they own "natural/clean," not "clinical." This is exactly the gap Clarte's "Imported Quality, Reachable Price" tagline is built for: same price tier, but credentialed (derma + clinical + manufacturing) rather than naturalistic. The threat is overlap on AOV and on bundle-led acquisition, NOT on positioning.
