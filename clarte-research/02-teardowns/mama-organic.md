## 1. Brand Snapshot
Mass-market "natural skin and hair care" brand operated by INTERLINK GLOBAL. Home headline: "Mama Organic is a Natural Skin and Hair Care Brand in Pakistan." Positioning is ingredient-led (Onion, Rosemary, Vitamin C, Niacinamide, Retinol, Tea Tree, Ubtan) at sub-Rs.2,000 unit prices — the opposite end of the price ladder from Clarte MD.

## 2. Landing Pages
No dedicated paid-traffic LPs detected. Funnel routes cold traffic to either the homepage, a category grid, or a single-product PDP. The PDP itself is the landing page — minimal above-the-fold scrolling required to reach Add-to-Cart.

**Uniform 15% discount across all 22 bundles** on /collections/offers-bundles — a flat, predictable bundling tax, not a tiered ladder.

Anchor bundles closest to Clarte's Rs.5,000 zone:
- Rosemary Hair Growth Kit (3-piece): Rs.4,300 → **Rs.3,650** (15% off)
- Retinol Anti Aging Kit (FW + Serum + Cream): Rs.4,098 → **Rs.3,480** (15% off)
- Vitamin C Brightening Kit (3-piece): Rs.4,010 → **Rs.3,410** (15% off)

Entry-tier 2-piece bundles cluster at **Rs.2,050–Rs.2,480**.
No bundle crosses Rs.4,000 sale price. **Clarte's Rs.5,000 Radiance Ritual sits above Mama Organic's entire bundle ceiling** — a positioning gift if framed correctly (clinical vs. organic, not just "more expensive").
No bundle-builder/customizer mechanic. No "save Rs.X" copy — only crossed-out list price next to sale price.

## 8. Urgency / Scarcity
- **Live low-stock counter on PDP:** "Only 4 left in stock" (Retinol), "Only 64 left in stock" (Vitamin C Serum). Likely auto-populated from Shopify inventory — this is the single strongest conversion mechanic on the site.
- No countdown timer. No "X people viewing." No flash-sale banner. No exit-intent popup detected.
- COD + 4–5 day delivery framing acts as soft urgency (low-friction commitment).

## 9. Funnel Type
**Cold-traffic → PDP → ATC → cart → checkout** (Shopify default). No quiz, no consult, no email-gate, no LP intermediary. Cross-sell happens on-PDP via two stacked bundle offers:
- "Retinol Anti Aging Bundle" Rs.2,080 (2-piece)
- "Retinol Anti Aging Kit" Rs.3,480 (3-piece)

This is a textbook **single-hero-PDP funnel with on-page AOV-laddering** — exactly the mechanic Clarte MD's Radiance Ritual page should replicate.

## 10. Mobile UX
Shopify theme, mobile-first. Sticky top promo bar ("COD All Over Pakistan"). PDP collapses bullets into expandable sections; price + ATC sits within first scroll on mobile viewport. WhatsApp number is tap-to-call. No sticky ATC bar visible from extraction.

## 11. Clarte MD Strategic Implications
This brand is **not a positioning competitor** — they sit at Rs.1,400 mass-organic, Clarte MD sits at Rs.5,000 premium-clinical. The teardown value is purely **PDP conversion mechanics**.

**What to steal for Clarte's Radiance Ritual PDP:**
1. **Live low-stock counter** ("Only X left in stock") — the most copy-able element. Works because it's specific, dynamic, and non-cheesy. For a premium ritual at Rs.5,000, frame as "Limited launch batch — X kits remaining" to keep tone clinical, not Shopify-spammy.
2. **On-PDP bundle laddering** — show the 2-piece bundle and 3-piece kit *on the single-product PDP itself*, so visitors who land on a single-SKU page see the upsell to the full ritual without leaving.
3. **Numbered "How to use" block** directly under benefits — Mama Organic's 4-step format is the right shape for Clarte's morning/night ritual choreography.
4. **Keyword-loaded PDP title pattern** — "[Product] - [Size] For [Benefit] With [Hero ingredients]" — strong for SEO and for ad-click intent matching.

**What to explicitly NOT copy:** the flat 15% bundle discount (telegraphs it's always-on, kills urgency), the UGC-tone review quotes (wrong register for premium-clinical), the bare price (no anchor — Clarte needs anchor pricing to justify Rs.5,000).

**Positioning gift:** Mama Organic's bundle ceiling is Rs.3,650. Clarte MD at Rs.5,000 sits above the entire category, leaving room to occupy "clinical / dermatologist-grade" without overlap.
